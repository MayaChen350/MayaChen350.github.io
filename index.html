Welcome to my personal page! I’m an autistic nerdy girl who’s hyperfixated about programming, music, some video games and Toby Fox (my idol fr).I like embedded systems <3

I’m from Canada, Québec and I really like electronic rock. You don’t know electronic rock? Well it’s funny, In 2022 when Metal Gear Rising Revengeance became popular again I really liked the soundtrack. But there’s a song that really was giving me goosebumps everytime. Since i’m a kid I always liked electronic music and I loved dubstep for the 2-3 years it lasted,. I was really surprised when I discovered “Stains of time”, AKA Monsoon’s battle theme. Not only it was making the battle even more epic this entire song was a banger and it was making me shake from the inside how much i loved that song it fitted my weird tastes at 100%, I really wanted to find other songs like it but failed for a while. I then gave up thinking there was not anything else like it ever. In November 2023, I had found a spotify playlist rocktronic, and I fell on a song by Celldweller called “Electric Eye”. I was so confused about how good it was. I could not believe my ears, I went listen to other of his songs and I found really hard to realize: I loved so much of his songs. Celldweller became my source of happiness of goosebumps and all for months and it really made me discover what really is Electronic Rock. The song “End of an empire” is a really good exemple. It’s long but worth it, Celldweller’s 2nd album is my favorite btw. Also if you want different electronic “rock” I really recommend “The path of totality” by Korn. Really good album that I had never discovered before somehow. Even Skrillex made GOOD songs with them. It’s really insane. I hope you’ll like it,
